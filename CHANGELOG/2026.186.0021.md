# ServeCheckVerb collapse: kit candies get NewMeta, ending the SchemaFS/SchemaDir/InputDefs trio

sdk.ServeCheckVerb's signature collapses from (kv, calver, schemaFS, schemaDir, inputDefs) to
(kv, meta pb.PluginMetaServer) — the SAME (provider, meta) shape as sdk.Serve/sdk.Main. The internal
checkVerbMeta is deleted; a kit candy now exports its OWN NewMeta() (sdk.NewMeta with explicit caps +
its embedded schema), exactly like every pb-provider plugin, so both authoring shapes converge on
NewCheckVerb()/NewProvider() + NewMeta() (R3). The exported SchemaFS/SchemaDir/InputDefs trio is gone
(SchemaFS unexported, SchemaDir/InputDefs deleted — their data lives in NewMeta). Consumed by the
15-kit-candy migration + the registerCompiledCheckVerb(kv, meta) rewrite in the superproject.
