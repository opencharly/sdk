# sdk.VerbVerdict + sdk.Preview — collapse the live verbs' verdict pipeline + resultJSON copies

Every live check verb ran a byte-identical exit/stdout/stderr/artifact verdict block after
dispatching its op, and most carried a local `resultJSON`/`preview`/`pluginResult` copy of the
sdk equivalents. sdk.VerbVerdict(verb, method, out, runErr, op, artifact) hoists the whole
pipeline into ONE implementation (R3): runErr→exit/stderr, the op.ExitStatus compare, the
op.Stdout/op.Stderr MatchAll validators, the (conditional) artifact validators, and the pass/fail
{status,message} reply. sdk.Preview (the exported 400-char truncator) backs its messages and any
verb whose verdict genuinely differs (plugin-vm's libvirt verb keeps its captured-stderr composition
but now calls sdk.ResultJSON + sdk.Preview). Consumed by the 11-plugin migration in the superproject.
