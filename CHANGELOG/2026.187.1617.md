# Pod-template resolve wire type (Cutover J)

Extend the substrate-template de-type to `pod:` templates: candy/plugin-substrate
projects an authored pod TEMPLATE into a ResolvedPod the kernel consumes without
importing the concrete spec.Pod.

- `spec/substrate_template_wire.go`: `ResolvedPod`, `PodResolveInput/Reply`, and a
  `Pod` arm on the discriminated `SubstrateTemplateResolveRequest`.

No CUE-schema change (the authored `#Pod` shape is untouched) — no `#SchemaVersion`
bump, no gen-repro drift.
