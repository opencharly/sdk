# 2026.200.2200 — CandyReader agent/terminal-profile accessors (W9 follow-on)

- Add `agent_provide`/`terminal_profiles` support to `spec.CandyReader`: a
  `terminal_profiles` field on `#CandyView` (mirroring the existing
  `agent_provide` field), plus `AgentProvide()`, `HasAgentProvides()`, and
  `TerminalProfiles()` interface methods, implemented on
  `deploykit.specCandyAdapter` and populated by `loaderkit.populateFromYAML`
  from the authored `agent_provide:`/`terminal_profile:` candy manifest keys.
- Closes the gap between the W9 `CandyReader` retype and the federated
  control-plane feature's OCI-label-collector consumers
  (`render_baked_metadata.go`'s per-box agent/terminal aggregation), which
  need this data without a live `*Candy`.
