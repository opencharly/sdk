# 2026.189.1740 — sdk/deploykit: InstallPlan step vocabulary (P4-unit-3)

Introduces sdk/deploykit and moves the InstallPlan step VOCABULARY out of charly:
the 13 concrete InstallStep implementations (SystemPackagesStep/BuilderStep/OpStep/
FileStep/ServicePackagedStep/ServiceCustomStep/ShellHookStep/ShellSnippetStep/
RepoChangeStep/ApkInstallStep/LocalPkgInstallStep/RebootStep/ExternalPluginStep) +
their field-structs (RepoSpec/CacheMountSpec/ArtifactRef) + the pure classification
helpers (reverseFileKindFor internal; OpStepScope/PathIsSystemScoped exported for
charly). deploykit imports sdk/spec (the IR enums + InstallStep interface + InstallPlan
container). Each concrete step implements spec.InstallStep structurally. The
plugin-dispatch externalStep cluster stays charly-side. Third P4 unit — the step
vocabulary becomes importable by any out-of-tree deploy plugin.
