# Resource resolve wire types (Cutover G)

Support the resource de-type: candy/plugin-resource resolves an authored `resource:`
(an exclusive-host-resource claim — a GPU vendor selector) into a ResolvedResource the
kernel's GPU arbiter consumes without importing spec.Resource.

- `spec/resource_wire.go` (hand-written): `ResolvedGpuSelector`, `ResolvedResource`,
  `ResourceResolveInput/Reply`.

No CUE-schema change (the authored `#Resource` shape is untouched) — no `#SchemaVersion`
bump, no gen-repro drift.
