# 2026.189.1400 — sdk/spec: InstallPlan IR discriminator enums (P4-unit-1)

Moves the InstallPlan IR discriminator enums out of charly into sdk/spec
(`ir_enums.go`, hand-written): `Venue` (int, +String), `Phase` (int, +String),
`StepKind` (string, 13 kinds), `Gate` (string, 4 gates). These are INTERNAL
execution enums, not wire carriers — the StepView wire struct (deploy_wire.go)
serializes them as primitives (`Venue int`, `Gate string`, `Kind string`), so the
wire-type-CUE mandate does not apply, and `Venue`/`Phase` are int iota enums
`cue exp gengotypes` cannot faithfully express anyway. Hand-written matches the
`ReverseOpKind`/`Scope` precedents already in spec. First P4 unit — the IR
E-envelope begins its move to sdk/spec; the InstallPlan struct + step vocabulary follow.
