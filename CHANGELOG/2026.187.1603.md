# Substrate-template resolve wire types (Cutover I)

Support the local + android substrate-template de-type: candy/plugin-substrate
projects an authored local:/android: TEMPLATE into a Resolved* envelope the kernel
consumes without importing the concrete spec.Local / spec.Android.

- `spec/substrate_template_wire.go` (hand-written): `ResolvedLocal`, `ResolvedAndroid`
  (+ its `IsEndpoint`/`EffectiveSerial` methods), `LocalResolveInput/Reply`,
  `AndroidResolveInput/Reply`, and the discriminated `SubstrateTemplateResolveRequest`.

No CUE-schema change (the authored `#Local`/`#Android` shapes are untouched) — no
`#SchemaVersion` bump, no gen-repro drift.
