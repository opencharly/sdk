# Venue-hop descent-descriptor (Cutover H, part 1)

The deploy chain's venue-hop selection stops switching on the substrate kind word.

- `schema/deploy.cue`: a loader-DERIVED `descent?: #DescentDescriptor` field on
  `#Deploy` — `{transport: none|container-exec|ssh|reject, host_rooted?}`. Never
  authored (rejected by `#DeployValue`'s `descent?: _|_` in `node.cue`); the
  transport SET is the kernel's closed nesting-boundary vocabulary.
- `kit/descent.go`: `StampDescent(*spec.Deploy)` — the SINGLE source of the
  substrate-word → nesting-transport mapping (R3), recursing the nested/peer
  subtree. The kernel calls it; the mapping lives here, out of the kernel.

No `#SchemaVersion` bump (the field is loader-derived, never an authored wire key);
gen-repro idempotent.
