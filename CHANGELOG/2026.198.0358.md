`sdk#64` (the shared 9-function resolver extraction) does not carry:

- `#EnsureImageRequest`/`#EnsureImageReply` (`sdk/schema/seam.cue`) — the F11

  fallback (after the two portable `kit.LocalImageExists`/`kit.TransferImage`

  `force_tty&&!isTerminal()`) since the plugin is a subprocess whose own
  stdout is not the operator's terminal.
